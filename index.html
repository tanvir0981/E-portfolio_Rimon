About the Founder

A Legacy of Excellence

Md. Akhtarul Islam Rimon, a visionary entrepreneur from Chapainawabganj, holds an Honors degree in Accounting. After gaining valuable field-level experience through several private sector roles, he transformed his expertise into a thriving business venture. In 2016, he founded Ayan International.

Initially focused on importing rice mill machinery, the company quickly expanded into five sectors — agriculture, packaging, fisheries, fruits & vegetables, and motorcycle sales & service. Guided by integrity, strategic thinking, and a commitment to sustainable growth, Mr. Rimon achieved significant milestones, including the prestigious recognition as a “Best Young Taxpayer.”

With nearly 14 years of industry experience, he has established himself as a prominent figure in the business community. His guiding principles are to uphold honesty in all business dealings and foster new entrepreneurs to strengthen Bangladesh’s economic growth.
